Educational Opportunities

Great Lakes Region Field Experience.
This award-winning cultural engagement and educational seminar can help students explore and understand the worldview of the Anishinaabeg (also known as Ojibwe), one of the largest aboriginal communities in North America. Participants will be immersed for 16 days in the history, culture, and lifeways of the Red Lake, Leech Lake, and White Earth Ojibwe Nations in northern Minnesota (near the Ontario-Manitoba border).

Summer Study Abroad in Tanzania.
Field experience may include visits to the Maasai of Tanzania, or other areas. Participants can learn from leading educators, traditional elders, medicine men, and women. They can experience family social life by living with local families, and visit historical sites and find out firsthand how culture, knowledge, language, and learning in people’s lives are intertwined.
